Means of Communication

We decided to mainly use Facebook Messenger as a way of communication between the two of us. Since Marin has a server, we will use that as a medium for all our work, since gitlab is limited in some regards. When big changes happen we will push to git.

Major decisions will be decided by the two of us, if we couldnt reach a conclusion on a topic, we will use Steve Blotts opinion on the matter to decide how we will continue.


Meeting Each Other

When it comes to work like major design work, how we treat data and major changes to the application, we usually meet up in person and to work together, to finish all major changes. Meeting each other
